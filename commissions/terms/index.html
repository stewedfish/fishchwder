terms

1. donations

  • All profits are completely donated to the current charity
  • Current charity + donation progress will be listed on the commissions page
  • Proof of donations will be consistently updated on the @fish.chwder Instagram account

2. ordering

  • Orders can be placed through the following google form
  • Within 24 hours, you will receive a DM request through the platform you specified on the order form from the shared fishchwder account
  • Your order will be confirmed as soon as the request is accepted
  • A rough sketch will then be sent, and once approved, an invoice will be made

3. payments

  • The invoice will be sent to the same email address specified on the order form
  • Payments will be processed through Square and can be done with either debit or credit card
  • Invoices must be paid completely before work on the commission can continue

4. communication

  • Communication with the artist will be handled entirely through the platform specified on the order form
  • Amount and frequency of updates sent depends on the complexity of the piece
    5. changes
  • All large changes (i.e. poses, composition, expressions, etc.) must be made clear and requested before the rough sketch is approved
  • After approval of the sketch, only minor adjustments can be requested (i.e. colours, background, etc.)

    • 6. wait times

  • Current estimated wait time for finished product: ~1 week
  • Depending on the complexity and amount of commissions in queue, a more accurate estimate will be given by the artist

    • 7. file formats

  • Once completed, PSD, PNG, and JPG versions of the work will be sent to the email address specified in the commission form
  • The PSD file will have all layers labelled and organized for easy access

    • 8. usage rights

    • Commissioned artwork entirely belongs to you, but cannot be used for unauthorized products (i.e. NFTs, merchandise, clothing, etc.)
    • Commissioned artwork may be uploaded to our social media with a watermark as an example or promotion for our cause
